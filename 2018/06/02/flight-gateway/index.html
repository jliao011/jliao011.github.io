    App shut down when idle

    When an app on Heroku has only one web dyno and that dyno doesn’t receive any traffic in 1 hour, the dyno goes to sleep.

    Dyno

    Accounts are given a base of 550 hours each month in which your Free dynos can run.

    Solution

    6 Easy Ways to Prevent Your Heroku Node App From Sleeping
